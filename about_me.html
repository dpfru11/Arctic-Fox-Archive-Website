Meet the Developer

My name is Daniel. I am a student at Purdue University studying Computer Science with a minor in Philosophy. Growing up in a family with two other software engineers, I always knew I wanted to follow the same path. I grew up in Lafayette surrounded by two parents and two older brothers, one of which pursued his career in software engineering here as well. I decided to attend Purdue because I felt the most at home near the campus belltower, listening to the fight song every day at 5 O'clock!

A few fun facts about me:
I own a pet hamster named "Sushi".
I am fluent with the Trumpet.
I have memorized up to around 50 digits of pi.
